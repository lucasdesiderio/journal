## What I Work On

I enjoy the layer where product needs meet engineering reality: systems integration, backend development, cloud platforms, and the quiet decisions that make software easier to operate over time.

Recent public activity on LinkedIn also points to a strong interest in AI and conversational interfaces, especially the engineering work required to make real-time systems feel reliable, fast, and genuinely useful.

## What You'll Find Here

This site is for writing that sits between a notebook and a handbook. Expect posts about:

- software architecture and tradeoffs
- integration-heavy systems and operational lessons
- cloud and backend engineering
- experiments, notes, and technical references I want to keep close at hand

## A Bit More Context

According to my public LinkedIn profile, I studied at Fundacao Getulio Vargas and work comfortably across Portuguese, English, and Spanish. That mix of technical depth, business context, and communication is a big part of how I like to build and collaborate.

I care about clear thinking, maintainable systems, and sharing what worked well enough that someone else can move faster because of it.
